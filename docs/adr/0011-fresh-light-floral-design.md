# ADR-0011: Fresh, light visual identity with floral accents

**Status:** Accepted (2026-07-05)

## Context
The book's structure is the template, but its visual identity is dark, moody
photography. The owner explicitly wants "something fresh, with light floral
accents" instead.

## Decision
Light, airy theme throughout. Floral/botanical accent motifs (a nod to the
book's fruit-and-flower imagery, reinterpreted light). High-legibility recipe
pages — readable on a phone propped at the stove. Not the book's dark look.

## Consequences
- One theme; no dark-chrome/light-content split to maintain.
- Accent illustration/motif assets need sourcing or generating during build.
